Frequently Asked Questions

If your question is not here, write a forum post or try contacting us.

How do I make an account?

On every web page there is a "Sign In" button in the top right corner. On that page, there is a "Sign Up" button. Click that and make an account!

What is the general forum for?

Share any of your own development on the general forum. Additionally, use the general forum when your question does not fall under any one particular category.

What is the account for?

The account will allow you to answer questions and post questions on the forum.

What should I do next?

If you have understood all the information on this website. You are well on your way in becoming a proficient web developer! Take a look at our example CV site for some inspiration, or learn about database integration.
